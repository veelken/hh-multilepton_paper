\title{Search for Higgs boson pair production in final states with electrons, muons, and hadronically decaying $\PGt$ leptons at $\sqrt{s} = 13$~\TeV}

\date{\today}

\abstract{
  The results of a search for Higgs ($\textrm{H}$) boson pair production in final states with multiple electrons, muons, or hadronically decaying $\tau$ leptons is presented.
  The search is based on proton-proton collision data recorded by the CMS experiment at $\sqrt{s} = 13$~\TeV center-of-mass energy,
  corresponding to an integrated luminosity of $137.2$~fb$^{-1}$.
  Seven distinct event categories, distinguished by the multiplicity of electrons or muons ($\ell$)
  and hadronically decaying $\tau$ leptons ($\tau_{\textrm{h}}$) are included in the analysis:
  %% $0\ell + 4\tauh$, $1\ell + 3\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, $2\ell$ of same-sign charge, $2\ell + 2\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, $3\ell$, $3\ell + 1\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, and $4\ell$.
  $2\ell$ of same charge, $3\ell$, $4\ell$, $3\ell + 1\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, $2\ell + 2\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, $1\ell + 3\tau_{\textrm{h}}$, and $4\tauh$.
  These final states target Higgs boson pair ($\textrm{HH}$) production in which the pair decays into either $\textrm{WWWW}$, $\textrm{WW}\tau\tau$, or $\tau\tau\tau\tau$,
  and the $\textrm{W}$ bosons and $\tau$ leptons subsequently decay either leptonically or hadronically.
  No evidence for a signal is found in the data, and upper limits on the cross section for non-resonant as well as resonant $\textrm{HH}$ production from heavy particle decays are set.
  For non-resonant $\textrm{HH}$ production with event kinematics predicted by the Standard Model (SM),
  the observed (expected) upper limit on the production rate at $95\%$ confidence level (CL) is $XX$ ($XX$) times the SM cross section.
  The results also exclude regions in the plane of the $\textrm{H}$ boson coupling to the top quark ($\mathrm{y}_{\mathrm{t}}$) and of the trilinear $\textrm{H}$ boson self-coupling ($\lambda$).
  Assuming $\mathrm{y}_{\mathrm{t}}$ has the magnitude expected in the SM, the Higgs self-coupling is constrained to be in the interval $X.X < \lambda < X.X$.
  The observed (expected) limits on resonant $\textrm{HH}$ production cross section range from $XX$ to $XX$ ($XX$ to $XX$)~\pb at $95\%$ CL, 
  depending on the heavy decaying particle mass and spin (0 or 2).
}
